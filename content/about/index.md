I'm John Zdanowski. I spent years as a sonar engineer learning to separate signal from noise. Now I do the same thing with small business finances.

I co-founded [Phoenix Strategy Group](https://phoenixstrategy.group) with David Metzler, where we help growth-stage companies see their finances as a dynamic system — not a stack of reports nobody reads. I also co-founded [BrightZen](https://brightzen.com) and [Weekly Accounting](https://weeklyaccounting.com) with Jeff Abrams.

I created the Integrated Financial Model (IFM), the SEQ framework, the Contribution Engine, and the Fourth Statement — tools designed to give small businesses the same financial clarity that large companies take for granted.

I'm the author of *Zen and the Art of Weekly Accounting* (with Jeff Abrams), and I'm writing Book 2 now.

I live in Del Mar, California. I think best on walks, at fire pits, and in the car — almost never at a desk.

- **Financial frameworks** — IFM, SEQ, the Fourth Statement, the Contribution Engine
- **Weekly Accounting** — why the cadence matters
